## James periodicity
$P_k^{k+m}$ periodic in $k$ of period $\phi(m)$, where $\phi(m)$ is the number of integers $s$ such that $s\equiv 0,1,2,4\mod 8$ and $1\le s\le m$.

$P_k^{k+m}$ is the Thom space of $k\lambda_m$ over $RP^m$.
